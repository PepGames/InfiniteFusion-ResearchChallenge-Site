    How to Play

    Learn the rules of the Fusion Researcher Challenge.

    Basic Rules

    You are a Researcher studying Fusion Technology. Your goal is to acquire as much data on Fusion Pokémon as possible. You can achieve this goal by completing Research Assignments for the Research Lab.

    • Start by logging your Starter Pokémon as your first catch.
    • Keep track of each of your actions using the Action Log in order to receive credit towards Research Assignments.

      Actions you can keep track of include:

      • Catching

        A "Catch" Includes Wild, Starter, Gift, or Trade Pokémon. Adding a Catch to your Action Log requires 1 Catch Token.

      • Deaths

        A "Death" occurs anytime a Pokémon faints. Logging a Death will remove that Pokémon from your lists, so skip Death Logging if you'd like an easier Challenge.

      • Fusions

        A "Fusion" combines 2 Pokémon you own together.

      • Splits

        A "Split" seperates 2 Pokémon you have fused currently. Splitting a Fusion Pokémon you own requires 1 Split Token.

      • Battles

        A "Battle" occurs at major story beats. This includes Rival Fights, Gym Battles, and the Elite Four + Champion.

      • Completing Research Assignments will reward you with Research Points which you can spend at the Shop to buy Catch Tokens or Split Tokens.

    • If you run out of Research Points and can't gain any more then your run is over.

    Advanced Rules

    The Fusion Researcher Challenge was created with the intention to be played as a Nuzlocke Style run. Below you will find an Official Ruleset for this kind of Advanced Challenge.

    • Pokémon are considered 'Dead' when they faint.

      Keep track of Pokémon Deaths using the Action Log.

    • Only catch a Pokémon if you have a Catch Token.

      The first Pokémon caught on a route/area always rewards 1 Research Point, which can be used to buy more Catch Tokens.

    • Give Nicknames to every Pokémon you catch.
    • (Optional) Use the built-in Level Caps.
    • (Optional) Don't use Items in Battle.
    • (Optional) Play on "set" mode.
    • (Optional) Losing in a battle resets the run.

      • These rules are all strictly self-imposed. The website will keep track of your progress based on your actions in the Action Log. Run progress is stored in your browser cache temporarily, for a more permanent save Export and Import your saves using the export/import run buttons.

    • If you run out of Research Points and can't gain any more, or you run out of Pokémon, then your run is over.
